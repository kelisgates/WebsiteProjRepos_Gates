Studying Tips to Give You Motivation

Here are some tips to keep your motivation for studying strong!!

Tips...

  • Create a studying schedule so that you are organized when approaching your work.
  • Find a good note taking technique for you and use something that makes you want to take notes with it (ex. ipad)
  • Have a set study enviroment because it allows you to be in a study mood whenever you are in that environment
  • Do the hardest task first because typically students like to stress and worry about when to do the hardest task, and in return procastinate on all their assigments.
  • Take Breaks so that your mind has time to relax!!
  • Ask for help from people around you, like your professor, peers, or tutors!!
