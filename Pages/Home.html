Goal For Students

The goal of this website is to provide tips and inspiration for students on how to navigate their college life. With the end result of the students leaving this website with a more hopeful outlook towards their college life.

Purpose of Website

The purpose of this website is to provide users with a lot of helpful tips ranging from where to find study spots to best school supplies to use!

Target Audience

This website is mainly for college students going to University of West Georgia, but it can be used by any kind of student in general.
