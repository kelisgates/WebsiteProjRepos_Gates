Hot Spots For Studying

Starbucks

Amazing spot to relax and get coffee while studing for exams or doing group studies!

UWG Library

The UWG Library is a great place for completeing assignments and forces you to focus on completeing the work. Also, the library is connected to a starbucks, so you can stop and grab a cup of coffee!!

Panera Bread

Panera Bread has a very nice and peaceful environment for studying. Also, they have a lot of nice snacks and meals for people with any kind of appetite!!
